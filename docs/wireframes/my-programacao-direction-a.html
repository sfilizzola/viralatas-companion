My Schedule

Mid-festival focus: ended picks stay on their day with inline status chips. No footer "Saw / Didn't See" buckets. Opt-out attendance unchanged. Tab rename approved. Ratings deferred.

Assumptions (Junior pass)

  • Within-day order A2: upcoming first, then ended rows at bottom of the same day section.
  • Attended = ended + not in missedBandIds (opt-out, no extra tap).
  • Missed = user tapped "I didn't see this band" in modal (unchanged flow).
  • Coach banner shows once per device, dismissible, first time any pick ends.
  • Pick paw hidden on ended rows (same as today’s hidePick in saw bucket).
  • Bottom nav label: "Schedule" — icon stays pick/paw.
